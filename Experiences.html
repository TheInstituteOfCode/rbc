School of Surf

Ride the waves along the iconic shores of Pantai Batu Bolong with a curated surf lesson led by internationally certified instructors from the Rip Curl School of Surf. Taking place just steps from the resort, each 1.5-hour session is tailored to all levels, from first timers learning essential techniques to experienced surfers refining their skills. Optional photo and drone captures are available to preserve each moment. Ideal for solo travellers or shared adventures, this immersive experience connects you to the rhythm of the sea.
